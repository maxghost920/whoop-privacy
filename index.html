Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

This is a personal-use integration that connects to the Whoop API to retrieve health and fitness data (recovery, strain, sleep, and workout metrics) for a single authorized user.

Data collected: Recovery scores, sleep performance, strain, heart rate, and workout data from your Whoop account.

Data usage: Data is stored locally and used solely for personal health tracking and analysis. It is not shared with any third parties.

Data storage: All data is stored on a private server controlled by the account owner. No data is sold, shared, or transmitted externally.

Data deletion: You can revoke access at any time through your Whoop account settings. All locally stored data can be deleted on request.

Contact: This is a personal project. For questions, reach the developer directly.
